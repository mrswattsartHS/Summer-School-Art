Feldman's 4 Steps of Art Analysis

Explore and interact with the structured approach to understanding art

1. Description

What do you see? Describe the artwork without interpretation. Focus on the facts: subject matter, medium, size, colors, shapes, and techniques used.

2. Analysis

How is the artwork organized? Examine the elements of art and principles of design. Look at composition, balance, contrast, rhythm, and focal points.

3. Interpretation

What is the meaning or message? Consider the mood, symbolism, and context. What do you think the artist is trying to communicate?

4. Judgment

Do you think the artwork is successful? Justify your opinion based on the previous steps. Consider its originality, emotional impact, and craftsmanship.
