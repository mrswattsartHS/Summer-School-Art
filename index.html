ART010: Summer Fine Arts Studio

Accelerated Level 2 Yearlong Course in 5 Weeks

This course includes detailed instructions, rubrics, and expectations. Access this in your learning platform.

📌 Studio-Based Course: All assignments must be completed by hand. Digital submissions will receive a grade of 0.
🎨 Supplies Required: Bring your own: paints, brushes, clay/playdough, crayons, markers, pencils, paper, coloring tools.
🎥 Webcam Policy: On-camera presence is required daily. Use a phone or alternate device if necessary.

🧑‍🎨 About the Course

This intensive, fast-paced art course is for highly motivated 11th and 12th graders. It blends art history, analysis, and studio work in a 5-week schedule.

  • 📚 Prerequisite: Level 1 Art / Foundations of Art
  • 🧠 Knowledge Required: Elements of Art, Principles of Design, Feldman’s Analysis
  • ⏱️ Expect 2+ hours of work per day

🗓️ Course Structure

  • ✍️ 2 TDA (Text-Dependent Analysis) Writing Assignments
  • 🎨 1 Major Studio Project
  • 📷 Daily On-Camera Progress Check-Ins
  • 🧪 Weekly Summative Assessments
  • ✅ Daily Reading Checks (retakes allowed)

⚠️ Note: Weekly units close permanently after the deadline.

🎥 Live Class Expectations

  • 🎓 Attend all live sessions

  • 🖼️ Bring work-in-progress for group feedback
